LITTLE DOG, BIG DAY!



It is a lazy Saturday morning, and Messi is lounging on the couch, peacefully drifting in an out of sleep. In the kitchen, his owner is making breakfast. As the scent begins to waft through the house, Messi begins to stir from his slumber. Right as his owner finishes plating the food, there is a knock at the door. Does Messi go examine the food or does he see who is at the door?
